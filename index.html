What We Can't Accept

  • Used pacifiers or teethers
  • Expired baby food, formula, or medication
  • Broken, cracked, or unsafe items (car seats past expiry, recalled products)
  • Torn, heavily stained, or tattered clothing, shoes, or books

If you're unsure, please reach out to Muthoni India on 0726 499 182 before dropping anything off.
