✅ What You Can Pass On

If you'd give it to a friend, it's perfect for Baraka Box!

  • Baby Essentials (0–2 yrs): Clothes, blankets, shoes, cots, strollers
  • Maternity Wear & Support: Dresses, belly bands, nursing bras
  • Feeding & Care: Bottles, sterilizers, bibs, breast pumps
  • Play & Learning: Toys, books, baby gyms
  • Bath & Hygiene: Towels, baby bathtubs, unopened toiletries

❌ What We Can't Take

We keep it safe and high-quality for all families.

  • Used pacifiers, expired food/formula, broken or unsafe items
  • Torn or tattered clothes, shoes, and books
